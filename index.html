Stamps of India

India boasts a rich and diverse history of postage stamps, reflecting the nation’s cultural heritage and historical milestones. The first postage stamp of India, known as the “Scinde Dawk,” was issued in the Sindh region in 1852. However, it wasn’t until 1854 that the first official postage stamp of British India, the “India Postage,” was released.
